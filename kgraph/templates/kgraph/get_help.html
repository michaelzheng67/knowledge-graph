Knowledge Graph

The Knowledge Graph is a personalized tool that helps you visualize and reinforce what you know. Each topic you add becomes a node in an interactive graph.

You can attach information to each topic and quiz yourself on it. When you answer questions correctly, the corresponding node stays vibrant. If you neglect a topic over time, the node gradually fades to represent memory decay.

By re-quizzing yourself on topics, you can revive fading nodes and strengthen your understanding again. This makes the graph a living map of your retained knowledge.
